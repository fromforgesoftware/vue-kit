# DateRangePicker

Composite range picker â€” segmented start/end date field or button trigger paired with a popover dual-month Calendar. Built on Reka UI's `DateRangePicker` primitives.

## When to use

- Filters and reports requiring a contiguous date range (e.g. last 7 / 30 days).
- Booking flows where check-in and check-out are visualised together.
- Forms with optional preset shortcuts via the `presets` prop.

## When not to use

- Single-date selection â€” use [DatePicker](?path=/docs/dates-datepicker--docs).
- Always-visible range â€” use [RangeCalendar](?path=/docs/dates-rangecalendar--docs).
- Month- or year-level granularity â€” use [MonthRangePicker](?path=/docs/dates-monthrangepicker--docs) or [YearRangePicker](?path=/docs/dates-yearrangepicker--docs).

- **3.3.1 Error identification**: use `error` + `describedBy` rather than colour alone.
- **2.4.7 Focus visible**: 2 px ring on field/trigger and on every cell.
- **2.5.8 Target size**: triggers and day cells meet â‰Ą 24 Ă— 24 px at every size.
