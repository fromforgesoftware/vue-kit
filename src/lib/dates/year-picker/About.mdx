# YearPicker

Composite year picker — button trigger that opens a popover with a paged grid of year cells. Built on Reka UI's `YearPicker` primitives.

## When to use

- Annual reports and KPI dashboards.
- Filters and forms where the smallest meaningful unit is a calendar year.
- Birth-year inputs and other historic-year selectors.

## When not to use

- Month or day granularity — use [MonthPicker](?path=/docs/dates-monthpicker--docs) or [DatePicker](?path=/docs/dates-datepicker--docs).
- Year _range_ selection — use [YearRangePicker](?path=/docs/dates-yearrangepicker--docs).

## Anatomy

<Canvas of={YearPickerStories.Default} />

`YearPickerRoot` wraps a header (page navigation + heading) and a 4-column grid of years. `yearsPerPage` controls how many years appear at once (default 12).

## Accessibility (WCAG 2.2 AA)

- **2.4.6 Labels**: pair every picker with `<Label for="id">`.
- **3.3.1 Error identification**: `error` + `describedBy`.
- **2.4.7 Focus visible**: 2 px ring on trigger, nav buttons, cells.
- **2.5.8 Target size**: trigger and year cells meet ≥ 24 × 24 px.
- **4.1.2 Name & state**: selected cell exposes `data-selected`; nav buttons carry `aria-label`s.
